Hashbrown Breakfast Lasagna

Ingredients Quantity
Valveeta Cheese (Sliced) As many slices as you want
Eggs 2 Large
Bacon (Cooked Crispy) 1/2 lb.
Sausage Crumbles (Cooked) Eyeball it :)
Parmesan Cheese 1/4 cup
Butter (melted) 1 tsp.
Mozzarella Cheese 1/3 cup
Hash Browns 1 package
Country Gravy 1 packet
Salt and Pepper To taste

Step 1: In a large bowl, combine the hash browns, eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper. Mix well.
Step 2: Add a single layer of hashbowns in the bottom of your pan and flatten it evenly.
Step 3: Make the gravy mix. Add more pepper if you'd like.
Step 4: Top the layer with sausage, bacon, valveeta, and gravy.
Step 5: Make a layer using the mozzarella cheese.
Step 6: Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
Step 7: Pour butter over top.
Step 1: Add remaining gravy to the top.
Step 1: Bake at 400 degrees for 25-30 minutes.

Pizza Casserole

Ingredients Quantity
Pizza Sauce 3-1/2 Cups
Elbow Noodles (or kind of choice) 1 box (16 ounces)
Pepperoni 8 Ounces
Parmesan Cheese 1/3 Cup
Mozzarella Cheese 4 cups (shredded)
Large Onion 1 (chopped)
Ground Beef 2 lbs.

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Step 2: In a large skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.
Step 3: Stir in spaghetti sauce and pasta.
Step 4: Transfer into 2 greased 13x9-in. baking dishes.
Step 5: Sprinkle with cheese and arrange pepperoni over the top.
Step 5: Bake, uncovered, 25-30 minutes or until heated through.

Chicken Stir-Fry

  • Time: 30 Minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 Minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Dark Sesame oil 2 tablespoons
Garlic Cloves (finely minced) 2 cloves
Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breast 2 lbs.
Broccoli 1 head
Mushrooms (sliced) 1 dozen
Carrots (julienned) 3 large carrots
Green Beans (diced) 1/4 lb.
bok choy (chopped) 1 head
Teriyaki Sauce 2-3 tablespoons

Step 1: Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a saute pan over medium heat. Add garlic and stir.
Step 2:Place the chicken in the pan and brown 4 minutes on each side. Remove from pan, slice into strips, set aside.
Step 3:Heat remaining tablespoon of oil in a wok over high heat.
Step 4:Add the vegetables and teriyaki sauce. Stir-fry quickly until the vegetables begin to soften.
Step 5:Add the chicken strips, combine well and continue to cook for 2 to 3 minutes. Serve immediately.
